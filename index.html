Happy Pups
Good friends!

Dogs are man's best friends. Like all relationships , it works best when both parties are healthy and happy. At HappyPup, our goal is to provide a platform through which dog owners and lovers, enthusiasts and medical practitioners monitor and care about their dogs' wellbeing, happiness and general health. We truly believe that dogs are a treasure that should be treated as such and provided the best care possible

About the Project
Inspiration

The project was inspired by man's love for dogs and pets in general. This is a one-stop for an effective dog companion app. It aims at keeping tabs on owners and their best friends. It serves also as a hub where one can come in and search up medical centres for dog treatment.
